The seed is blackish and round; four usually seem in a husk together: the root is long and stringy, with divers fibres thereat, and abides many years.

Place. It grows in woods, and by wood-sides; as also in divers fields and bye-lanes in the land.

Time. It flowers in June, July, and August.

Government and virtues. The herb is under Venus.
