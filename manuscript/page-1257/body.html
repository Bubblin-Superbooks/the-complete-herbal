syrupus Infusionis Florum Pæoniæ.

Or Syrup of the infusion of Peony flowers.

College. It is prepared in the same manner as Syrup of Clove-gilliflowers.

syrupus De Pæonia Compositus.

Or Syrup of Peony compound.

College.
