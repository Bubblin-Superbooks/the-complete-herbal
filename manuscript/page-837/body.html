Chapter V.

Of Barks.

  • Barks, which physicians use in medicine, are of these sorts: Of fruits, of roots, of boughs.
  • The barks of fruits are to be taken when the fruit is full ripe, as Oranges, Lemons, &c. but because I have nothing to do with exotics here, I pass them without any more words.
