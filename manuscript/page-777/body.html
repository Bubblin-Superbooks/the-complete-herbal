Place. It grows generally throughout this land in divers places of the hedges and way-sides, and other waste grounds.

Time. It flowers in July, and the seed is ripe soon after.

Government and virtues. This is an herb of Venus, and excellent for the womb to strengthen and remedy all the cold griefs of it, as Plantain doth the hot. Vervain is hot and dry, opening obstructions, cleansing and healing.
