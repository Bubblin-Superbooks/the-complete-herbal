The bark of the tree, and also the leaves, are excellently good, being used in fomentations for broken bones, and such members as are out of joint. Pliny saith, the branches of the tree defend houses from lightning, and men from witchcraft.

St. John’s Wort.

This is a very beautiful shrub, and is a great ornament to our meadows.

Descript.
