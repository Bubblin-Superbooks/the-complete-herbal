Place. This grows with us in gardens only, and other manured places.

Time. It is an annual plant, flowering in July, and the seed is ripe in August.

Government and virtues. It is an excellent sauce for such whose blood wants clarifying, and for weak stomachs, being an herb of Mars, but naught for choleric people, though as good for such as are aged, or troubled with cold diseases. Aries claims something to do with it, therefore it strengthens the heart, and resists poison.
