The Wallnut Tree.

It is so well known, that it needs no description.

Time. It blossoms early before the leaves come forth, and the fruit is ripe in September.

Government and virtues. This is also a plant of the Sun. Let the fruit of it be gathered accordingly, which you shall find to be of most virtues while they are green, before they have shells.
