Meadow Trefoil, Or Honeysuckles.

It is so well known, especially by the name of Honeysuckles, white and red, that I need not describe them.

Place. They grow almost every where in this land.

Government and virtues. Mercury hath dominion over the common sort. Dodoneus saith, The leaves and flowers are good to ease the griping pains of the gout, the herb being boiled and used in a clyster.
