Place. It grows wild in the fields about London in many places, as at Mile-End green, and many other places.

Time. It flowers early, and seeds in July, and sometimes in August.

Government and virtues. This, as almost all Thistles are, is under Mars. The seed of this Star Thistle made into powder, and drank in wine, provokes urine, and helps to break the stone, and drives it forth. The root in powder, and given in wine and drank, is good against the plague and pestilence; and drank in the morning fasting for some time together, it is very profitable for fistulas in any part of the body.
