The root consists of many small fibres, which grows not deep in the ground, but abides all the winter therein, shooting forth new branches every year, the old one lying down to the ground.

Place. It grows in the open places of woods and copses, on both moist and dry grounds, in many places of this land.

Time. It flowers about the month of July.
