Cowslips, Or Peagles.

Both the wild and garden Cowslips are so well known, that I neither trouble myself nor the reader with a description of them.

Time. They flower in April and May.

Government and virtues. Venus lays claim to this herb as her own, and it is under the sign Aries, and our city dames know well enough the ointment or distilled water of it adds beauty, or at least restores it when it is lost. The flowers are held to be more effectual than the leaves, and the roots of little use.
