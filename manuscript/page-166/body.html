There is another sort nothing different from the former, but that it is greater, and the flowers of a paler green colour.

Place. They grow in small standing waters, and usually near Water-Cresses.

Time. And flower in June and July, giving seed the next month after.

Government and virtues. It is a hot and biting martial plant. Brook-lime and Water-Cresses are generally used together in diet-drink, with other things serving to purge the blood and body from all ill humours that would destroy health, and are helpful to the scurvy.
